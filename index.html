Survive the Night

A New Survival Map for Left 4 Dead 2

HOLD OUT OVERVIEW

We freature a brand new experience within the map, allowing people to experience a hold out in their favorite subways, featuring rich props and features. cause remeber, Its not always sunny in the subways.

MAP DETAILS

01. Immersive Atmosphere

We the members of this comapny have been working on this map for over a couples of days, and we are proud to say that we have created a map that is both immersive and fun to play.

  • Custom dynamic lighting systems, with a focus on realism and atmosphere.
  • High-fidelity environmental textures, with a focus on realism and atmosphere.
  • Premium reserved quality, its like youre really punching!!

02. Strategic Level Design

We have designed the map to be a fun and challenging experience, with a focus on strategy and teamwork.

  • Innovative urban transit, with a focus on realism and atmosphere.
  • Efficient infrastructure and seamless connectivity, with a focus on realism and atmosphere.
  • Interactive environmental hazards, with a focus on realism and atmosphere.

03. Custom Scripted Events

we like to add unique character that people can interact with, such as the subway train, or the subway platform, And teletubbies.

  • good old fashioned gunplay
  • Dynamic space for unique interactions
  • Not so healthy citizens, you get to shoot
